To our wonderful guests

As we step into marriage, we do so with full hearts, knowing that this union is not only a celebration of love between two people but a testimony of God’s faithfulness and grace. This day is more than a wedding; it is a moment to give honor to the One who brought us together and who will forever be at the center of our marriage.

We are so excited to rejoice, worship, and give thanks with all of you. May our wedding be a reflection of God’s love — full of joy, peace, and His divine presence. We hope that through this celebration, you too will feel His goodness and faithfulness that have carried us to this beautiful day.

Thank you for walking with us, praying for us, and loving us. Your support and presence are the greatest gifts we could ever receive.

Love,
